---
title: CIEM
---

**Cloud Infrastructure Entitlement Management** (**CIEM**) "*are tools designed to manage and govern access to cloud resources by providing detailed visibility and control over cloud infrastructure identities.*" CIEM tools help organizations enforce the principle of least privilege (PoLP) by ensuring that users, applications, and services have only the necessary access to perform their functions.
